confluence-reporting:export

Full name:

org.bsc.maven:confluence-reporting-maven-plugin:5.0-rc2:export

Description:

Export a confluence page either in PDF or DOC

Attributes:

  • The goal is thread-safe and supports parallel builds.
  • Since version: 3.4.3.
